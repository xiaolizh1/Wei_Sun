Featured Research Portfolio

Direction I: Scalable Process Supervision for LRMs

Designed and launched EpicPRM, an automated data curation framework for process reward modeling. Instead of relying on brute-force Monte Carlo counts, it utilizes token perplexity (PPL) for exact rolling state probability analysis, combined with an adaptive binary search strategy. This reduced process labeling computation by 64.39% while exceeding standard PRM800K scaling efficiency with less than 10% data density.

Direction II: Model-Free Critic Assignment for GRPO

Engineered Key-Token Advantage Estimation (KTAE) to answer the fundamental challenge of granular credit-assignment in value-model-free Reinforcement Learning. By extracting token impact dynamics natively through Fisher's Exact Test and Information Gain, KTAE forces models to achieve enhanced convergence stability and shorter optimal responses without deploying a parameter-heavy Critic networks.
